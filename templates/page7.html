Help Center

Click Accounts to manage users. "Default User" is created automatically.

The Home page lists your news categories. Click any category card to browse articles. You can use keyboard arrows and enter key to navigate.

Use the RSS Manager page to add new "Custom Categories" and add specific RSS Feed URLs manually.

RSS Finder allows you to enter a website URL (e.g. wired.com) and scan for feeds. You can then add them directly to a category.

On any article, click the dropdown button next to "Read" to save it to a specific favorite folder.
